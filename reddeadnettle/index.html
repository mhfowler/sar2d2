this web page is being served from a raspberry pi on a shelf inside of sari-sari

this raspberry pi also runs a scuttlebutt pub
(here is a more technical explanation if you're curious)

something beautiful about scuttlebutt is the pub just connects us and is easily subsituteable for another
(a form of resilience)

send me a message on any platform you can reach me
and I'll send you an invite which you can use to connect to the network
(a technical network, and also a community of people interested in resilience)

in the future you can also ask other people running pubs for invites so you don't depend on this pub for connectivity

even if there are no accessible pubs, scuttlebutt can also work via wifi, bluetooth and sneakernet,
using whatever connections are available

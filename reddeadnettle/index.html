this web page is being served from a raspberry pi (a small computer) on a shelf inside of sari-sari

this raspberry pi also runs a scuttlebutt pub

scuttlebutt is a decentralized social network,
and this pub is like one doorway into it
(here is a more detailed explanation if you're curious)

if you would like to join send me a message any way you can reach me
and I'll send you an invite which you can use to connect

once you are connected you can use it as you see fit,
but here I will share a little bit of what I appreciate about it

to me it feels like a learning group for community resilience

farming, fabrication, community organizing, permaculture, solarpower, sailing, mycology

I imagine larping that the global supply chain does not exist as a way to prepare for its collapse,
and to heal ourselves, decolonize, and become less alienated in the process

this learning group sometimes takes structured forms, a group of people intentionally studying a topic together, and sometimes it takes the form of self-study, sharing updates of what you are researching along the way

if you're interested in studying these topics together and connecting with people with similar interests,
(and slowly building a collaborative network of healing community spaces),
I hope this can be a doorway to facilitate that

i imagine wires connecting us directly that weave between and around the malls we often meet in

in the future you can also ask other people running pubs for invites
so you don't depend on this pub for connectivity either

* ps. I spent some time thinking about the name of this, I considered gardenkarass.network, queernaturestudies.network, communityresilience.network, and other names. I think community resilience is important, but I also feel resilience is nothing without softness, so this is a lil note for the intention of the doorway
